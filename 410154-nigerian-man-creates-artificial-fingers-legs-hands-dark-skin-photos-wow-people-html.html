Nigerian Man Creates Artificial Fingers, Legs, Hands for Dark Skin, Photos Wow People

- A young Nigerian artist, John Amanam, has put his skills into good use and created prosthetics that are fit for black people

- Photos of his artworks have gone viral, with people saying they found it amazing

- A Twitter user, @DesrineEnnis, spoke about how using John's piece gave her a new life after 20 years

As the years roll, it has become evident that talents among people have also been notching new heights.

John Amanam got massive media attention with his sterling craftmanship.

He created prosthetics for people with dark skin. This is amazing!

Photos shared by Twitter handle @Blacksregion showed the artificial hands and fingers he created that look perfect.

You would almost mistake it for real body parts. Many people who commented on the photos said if they were placed side by side with the natural parts, they would not be able to tell them apart.
