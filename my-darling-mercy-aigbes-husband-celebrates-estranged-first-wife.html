'My Darling'- Mercy Aigbe's Husband Celebrates Estranged First Wife

Nollywood movie marketer and husband of actress Mercy Aigbe, Kazim Adeoti has showered sweet words on his estranged wife, Oluwafunso Asiwaju.

Naija News reports that the estranged senior wife of the marketer turned 48 today, October 6th.

Taking to Instagram, Kazim shared a photo of Oluwafunso Asiwaju with beautiful words.

He prayed for her, saying; “Happy glorious birthday to you beautiful. Wishing you the happiest and the brightest day ever!. God bless you my darling Arike ❤️
asiwajucouture”.

Oluwafunso also took to her Instagram page to share stunning photos of herself dressed in Yoruba attires captioning the photos; “Chapter #48 I’m so grateful Lord. Happy Birthday to me”

“I dedicate this day to the Almighty God, who has kept me, and brought me this far. I wish myself a better now than ever. May God’s infinite power be upon me and grant me a life that doesn’t end with regret. Happy birthday to me”.
