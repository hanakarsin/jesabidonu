Kanye Wests Ex, Julia Fox Shares How Their Relationship Affected Her Career

American rapper and music producer, Kanye West’s model ex, Julia Fox has opened up about her relationship with the ex-billionaire.

According to the reports, the 32-year-old model said her relationship with the business mogul took a negative toll on her career as an actress.

The duo had met last Christmas and only dated for about 6 weeks. Although their relationship was short-lived, they did make a statement on and off social media.

“After the big relationship, I definitely noticed a shift in an acting way,” she said.

“I’m not getting as many offers as I was before, weirdly. There’s been a lot of weird drawbacks with reaching that level of notoriety.”
