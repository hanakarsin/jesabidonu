Premier League transfer window: List of completed summer moves 2019

Football is one of the most enjoyed and accommodating sports in the world. It unites all countries, thus bringing global cohesiveness. This year’s Premier League transfer window is one that was eagerly awaited for by world’s top football clubs. It will allow them to select new players for their next season.

When did the Premier League transfer window open? The Premier League transfer window for the summer opened on May 16th 2019 and is set to close on August 8th 2019. However, International transfers of players that is those from another national association are yet to take off. This is until the FIFA Transfer Matching System (TMS) opens on June 11th.

Football transfer window

How does the transfer window work? A transfer window as it is referred to by the media is the exchange of players among the worlds recognized football clubs. However, this is not just a give and take program. Thousands of millions are exchanged between the clubs as they bid for the player of their choice. The process is, however, regulated by FIFA in which it opens the movement for two seasons in a year. The winter season and the summer.

During the player movements, the player, agent and the interested club present their lawyers who draft a new contract which includes the player’s salary and bonuses. They must also be physically fit to play for their new club. This is why they undergo a medical check-up which can affect the fee if proven not fit. Breach of this contract by either side is also a great offence, and it is taken with utmost seriousness.
