Singer Kizz Daniel Arrested In Tanzania (Video)

Nigerian award-winning singer, Oluwatobiloba Daniel Anidugbe popularly known as Kizz Daniel has been arrested in Tanzania for not performing at his concert.

Naija News reports that a video of his arrest which surfaced online captured the singer being escorted into the Police van.

The singerâ€™s fans in Tanzania had mobilised the police to arrest him over his alleged refusal to perform at his concert on Sunday, 7th August 2022.

The singer who arrived in the country earlier in the day reportedly refused to perform due to the unavailability of his clothes.

Some of the fans who took to Twitter to express their anger said some people who paid as much as $5000 for tables, later ripped the hall apart in frustration.

This is the third time Kizz Daniel is being called out by fans over the cancellation of his shows in the last month.

The father of two was first dragged on July 7 after he stood fans up for over five hours at his concert in DMV, United States.

He was also dragged by his fans after he cancelled his Denver concert abruptly on July 28, 2022.
