Samidoh Denies Using Cheap Publicity, Drama to Promote New Songs: &quot;Hakuna Kiki&quot;

  • Samidoh has denied chasing clout to create a buzz before releasing his music
  • The famous Mugithi artiste said he only uses his music to remain relevant
  • He also said he will continue to use his music to remain relevant
  • The musician trended for hours on the day before he released his new song 'Muthoniwa'

Singer Samidoh Muchoki has denied using cheap publicity stunts to promote his new songs.

On August 10, drama erupted after the singer's baby mama Karen Nyamu accused him of attacking her and breaking her phone.

Samidoh has never addressed these claims but released his new single called 'Muthoniwa' on August 13.

The song now has gathered over five hundred thousand views.

Samidoh: I will keep hitting the headlines, but with music

Speaking at an interview with Mungai Eve, the singer said he would continue to use his music to remain relevant to his fans and not publicity stunts.

"Hatujafika hapo. Kiki iko mbali sana na ile kitu, unajua ile kitu ilikuleta ama ilikuinua utaihitaji kukuweka pale. So wewe hukunijua juu ya kiki, ulinijua juu ya mziki so hiyo mziki ndio tu imeniweka pale pale. So hizi vichwa za habari nitaendelea kuzigonga an mziki sio hiyo mambo yote (We have not gotten to this. You know what raises you to a higher level, you will still need it to maintain that level. So I will keep hitting headlines, but with music not these other things.)," he said at the interview

Woman interrupts Samidoh's performance to ask about Karen Nyamu

TUKO.co.ke previously reported a lady Interrupted the singer's performance to inquire about Karen Nyamu.

In a video by Plug TV on Instagram, the singer could be seen performing on stage at a recent event. A lady fan recording the performance decided to interrupt the musician with a question regarding his family life.

In the clip, one could hear the fan asking him, in Kikuyu language, about baby mama Karen Nyamu's whereabouts.

"Samidoh where did you leave Karen? Is she taking care of the kid," she asked in Kikuyu.

Samidoh was lost for words as he smiled with his guitar in his hand and just went back to his microphone position.
