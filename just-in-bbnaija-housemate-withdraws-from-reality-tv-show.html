BBNaija Housemate Withdraws From Reality TV Show

KayVee, one of the male housemates in the Big Brother Naija show, season 6 has officially withdrawn from the show.

Naija News reports that this was announced by Big Brother on Monday.

According to Biggie, Kayvee is unwell and will not be able to continue the show.

Recall that other housemates like Cross, Whitemoney, JMK had complained about the housemate refusing to talk to anyone and acting strangely.

The photographer has mostly kept to himself, often looking lost in thought while in the house.
