Gus Barry - Age, Family, Bio

Young British actor who starred in the 2014 thriller Black Sea. He has been seen on television in Hetty Feather, Wolf Hall and an episode of Showtime's Penny Dreadful.

Before Fame

His on-screen debut came in 2013 when he cast alongside David Tennant in BBC's The Escape Artist.

Trivia

He played the role of Jem in the Regents Park award-winning production of To Kill a Mockingbird. Throughout his career, he has made a dozen different appearances on different TV series' and in films. His role as Mathias in Hetty Feather has been his longest-running character, with him appearing in over 30 episodes of the show.

Family Life

He is from the U.K.

Associated With

He starred alongside Jude Law in Black Sea.
