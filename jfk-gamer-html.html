JFK Gamer - Age, Family, Bio

Versatile YouTuber most well known for playing games like Call of Duty: Black Ops for his more than 210,000 subscribers.

Before Fame

He created his channel and posted his first Call of Duty video in November 2010.

Trivia

He has a second Let's Play channel peppered with vlogs and gameplay from Assassin's Creed and Star Wars: Battlefront.

Family Life

His real name is Jordan Gustafson. He has a younger sister. He has featured his girlfriend on his Instagram.

Associated With

In August 2015, he posted the video "Kino Der Toten - FIRST ROOM CHALLENGE" with fellow gamers Matt Cobuzio and Patrick Smith.
