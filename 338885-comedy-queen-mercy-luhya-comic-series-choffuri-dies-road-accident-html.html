Comedy queen Mercy of Luhya comic series Choffuri dies in road accident

- Mercy Nyukuri died on the night of Wednesday, February 6

- She was knocked down by a speeding vehicle while crossing the road

- Her body was taken to Life Care Hospital in Bungoma county

- Nyukuri was known for her role in the comic Bukusu Choffuri series

Queen of Luhya comic series Choffuri, Mercy Justine Nyukuri has died.

The comedian's death was confirmed by the lead member of the series, Tinny Black, who said her body was lying at the Life Care Hospital, Bungoma county.

Nyukuri is said to have succumbed to injuries sustained after she was knocked down by a speeding vehicle at around 10pm on Wednesday, February 5.

"Confirmed, she is dead, her body is lying at Life Care Hospital formerly Lumboka Hospital mortuary," said Black.

According to Black, popularly known as Choffuri (a corrupted version of how the Luhya pronounce the name Geoffrey), she was crossing the road when the vehicle knocked her.

"My beloved Auntie my best actress one who touched hearts of Luhya people with her amazing acting skills, l never thought l will write something like this about you," Black wrote on Facebook"But here is the begin of 2020, the second, why did you left without saying bye? How will l tell your fun's? But anyway R.I.P," he added

Choffuri who is well known for his work mostly done in his native Bukusu dialect and Swahili, hails from Matisi village, Webuye West constituency, in Bungoma county.
