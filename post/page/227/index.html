'Two Wrongs Don't Make A Right'

published on March 29, 2024
The Labour Party’s presidential candidate for the 2023 general election,Peter Obi, has criticized claims of reprisal attacks by the Nigerian Army over the killing of Nigerian army officials in Okuoma, Bomadi LGA, Delta. Condemning the retaliatory attack in a statement made available on his official X account, Obi suggested that a retaliatory attack on the community cannot compensate for the death of the soldiers. &quot;I Never Meant It That Way&quot;

published on March 29, 2024
The Governor of Benue State, Samuel Ortom has made a public apology after his viral statement that he won’t support a Fulani man to become the President of Nigeria. Speaking on Wednesday in Bauchi State, Ortom said his statement might have been taken out of context. It would be recalled that Ortom on Sunday while playing host to the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) G5 Governors in Benue State, said he would not be supporting the presidential candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), Atiku Abubakar. 56-year-old Widow Nabbed With Cocaine In Her Footwear

published on March 29, 2024
The National Drug Law Enforcement Agency (NDLEA) has confirmed the arrest of a woman at airports with cocaine. Naija News understands that the 56-year-old suspect, Ajisegiri Kehinde Sidika, had concealed the contraband substance in her footwear. The mother of four, who is also said to be a widow, was nabbed on Sunday (today) at the Murtala Muhammed International Airport, Lagos, while attempting to traffic 400g of cocaine concealed in her footwear to Makkah, Saudi Arabia.
