Wike Inaugurates First Set Of 100,000 Special Assistants

published on May 24, 2024
The Rivers State Governor Nyesom Wike, on Thursday, inaugurated the first set of the 100,000 special assistants he recently appointed to take charge of political units ahead of the 2023 general elections. Naija News reports that the governor while congratulating the aides said they should see it as an opportunity to serve in one of the most prestigious states in the country. Wike told his special aides at the inauguration at the Adokiye Amiesimaka stadium, Omagwa in Ikwerre local government area of the state that henceforth the decision concerning the direction of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) in the Rivers lies in their hands.
