Lawyer Donald Kipkorir criticised for flaunting his Range Rover

- Lawyer Donald Kipkorir has been criticised after posting photos of his new Range Rover Sport HSE (2016 model)

- Senior Counsel Paul Muite was one of the people who felt that Kipkorir's posts were uncalled for as no one was interested in knowing what he owned

Flamboyant city lawyer has been heavily criticised after sharing photos of his top of the range vehicles on social media.

Lawyer Donald Kipkorir, on Thursday, August 4, posted photos of his latest acquisition - Range Rover Sport HSE 2016 model.

The post was accompanied by a photo of the vehicle which is still in the RMA Motors showroom.
