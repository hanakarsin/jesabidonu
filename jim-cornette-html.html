Jim Cornette - Age, Family, Bio

Sports businessman known for his work in the world of professional wrestling. He owned Smoky Mountain Wrestling during the 1990s and worked for World Wrestling Entertainment and World Championship Wrestling. He was inducted into the New England Pro Wrestling Hall of Fame in 2015.

Before Fame

His fascination with wrestling began when he was a child; by his early teens, he was working as an announcer for live wrestling matches.

Trivia

He was the executive producer for an HDNet program called Ring of Honor Wrestling. He has garnered over 200,000 followers on his Twitter account, where he posts wrestling news and comedic content. 

Family Life

He married wrestler Stacy Goff (also known as "Synn") in late 2007.

Associated With

He managed the career of wrestler Adam "Scrap Iron" Pearce.
