Jessica Hatch - Age, Family, Bio

One-half of the husband and wife YouTube duo Gone to the Snow Dogs who operates the channel alongside her husband Jamie Hatch. Together, they share their love of Siberian Huskies featuring their dogs Shelby, Memphis, Oakley and the late Shiloh for more than 1.3 million subscribers.

Before Fame

She and Jamie are high school sweethearts. They created their YouTube channel in November of 2009.

Trivia

She and Jamie operate a side Snow Dogs Vlogs channel that had more than 75,000 subscribers as of August of 2016. Their shared Instagram account has more than 240,000 followers.

Family Life

She and Jamie were together for five years before getting married. They lived in Arizona for a couple years and would reside permanently in Michigan.

Associated With

She and Jamie are a husband and wife YouTube duo like Ellie and Jared.
