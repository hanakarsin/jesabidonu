Fire dance moves from firefighters go viral online

- The #JerusalemaChallenge has made its way to Portugal

- A video of three Portuguese firefighters dancing to the song made it's way to a Facebook group and has since gone viral

- The video has gained 738 000 views since it has been shared to the group

A video of firefighters in Portugal doing the #JerusalemaChallenge has made it's way to the #ImStaying Facebook group.
