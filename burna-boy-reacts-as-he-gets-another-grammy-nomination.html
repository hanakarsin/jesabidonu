Burna Boy Reacts As He Gets Another Grammy Nomination

Nigerian singer, Damini Ebunoluwa Ogulu, popularly known by his stage name, Burna Boy is excited after his album got nominated for the 2021 Grammy Award in the ‘Best Global Music Album’ category.

The singer’s album, ‘Twice As Tall got a nomination in the list which was released Tuesday afternoon, 24th November.

Others nominated alongside Burna Boy for the award include Antibalas (Fu Chronicles), Bebel Gilberto (Agora), Anoushka Shankar (Love Letters), and Tinariwen (Amadjar).

Reacting to the nomination, Burna Boy took to his Twitter account where he simply wrote: “Another one ……. #TWICEASTALL.”

Naija News reports that the 2021 Grammy awards is scheduled to hold on January 31, 2021 and is expected to be hosted by South-African comedian, Trevor Noah.

The ceremony will be broadcast by CBS from Los Angeles on Sunday, Jan. 31, 2021.

Meanwhile, it will be recalled that last year, the 29-year old Nigerian singer Burna Boy also got a Grammy nomination with his ‘African Giant’ album in the ‘World Best Music Album’ category but lost out to the eventual winner, Angelique Kidjo.
