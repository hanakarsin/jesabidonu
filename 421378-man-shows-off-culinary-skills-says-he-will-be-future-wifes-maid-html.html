While showing that cooking is not only meant for the female gender, the man identified as Prince Obua shared on a Facebook group, a meal of egg and fish sauce he had prepared

Obua excitedly stated that his future wife wouldn't have to worry about serving meals as he got her covered.

His post read:

"Today is Sunday and I have prepared egg and fish sauce. My future wife is covered because I will be her maid and her everything."

In viral photos, Ouba highlighted how he prepared the sumptuously-looking meal.

He was captured from the point of grinding ingredients to the final stage of cooking.

The post attracted many comments from ladies: who were in divided opinion:

Vivienne Odunze commented:

" A man who knows how to cook can never appreciate a lady' s food."

"Any guy who can cook well, no matter what their wife cook they will still notice errors."
