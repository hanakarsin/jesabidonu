Sienna Wilson - Age, Family, Bio

A member of the real-life Bat family, she rose to fame with her father, Blake Wilson, appearing in videos on his insanely popular Vine account BatDad. After Vine's closure, she would continue to appear on the BatDad Facebook and YouTube channel.

Before Fame

Before finding success on Vine, the Wilson family lived and worked in Roswell, Georgia.

Trivia

Since the creation of the BatDad Facebook page, the account has earned more than 8 million followers.

Family Life

She is joined on the BatDad social accounts by her mother, Jen, and siblings, Ben, Taylor, and Kaya.

Associated With

In one of the videos on her family's channel, there is a clip of them playing the game Mario Kart 8 Deluxe.
