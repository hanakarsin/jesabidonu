January 10 Horoscope and Zodiac

Determined and intelligent, your natural abilities are well-suited for a variety of career paths, although choosing one may be difficult. Your gifts for communication and order would translate well into careers such as law, law enforcement and teaching. Similarly, your quick mind would be a good fit for the demanding world of business, finance or economics. If you find an issue worth dedicating yourself to, a career in the political arena may be rewarding, such was the case for Grigori Rasputin, who was also born on January 10th. If music is your passion, you may do well as a lyricist or song writer, much like Jim Croce, another of your celebrity birthday twins.
