Iman Abdulmajid - Age, Family, Bio

Somali high fashion model, cosmetic entrepreneur and occasional actress. As of 2010, Iman Cosmetics was generating profits above $25 million annually.

Before Fame

She moved to the U.S. after being discovered by a photographer at age 20.

Trivia

She is also an author of two books, 2001's I Am Iman and 2005's The Beauty of Color.

Family Life

She married her third husband, David Bowie, in 1992. They remained married until his death in 2016. She has two daughters named Alexandria and Zulekha.

Associated With

She was a favorite model of the late fashion designer Yves Saint-Laurent.
