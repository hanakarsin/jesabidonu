Jaliyah Monet - Age, Family, Bio

Popular social influencer who has amassed over 3.2 million subscribers to her Jaliyahma YouTube channel, where she posts a variety of fun videos, including challenges. She has also run social media accounts under the name Life of Jaliyah. The music video for her song "I Need You" with Funny Mike has received over 13 million views since it was released in October of 2018.

Before Fame

She signed up for her lifeof_jaliyah Twitter account in April of 2012.

Trivia

She has earned over 2.6 million followers to her jaliyahma Instagram account, where she posts different fashion looks. She released her first solo music video for her single "Like Me" in December 2019. 

Family Life

She has been in a relationship with fellow YouTuber Funny Mike and they've run a channel called The MJ Family together. She and Mike welcomed a daughter named Londyn in February 2019. She gave birth to their second child, a son, in July 2020. Her younger sisters Jayla and Liyah have appeared on the MJ Family YouTube channel.

Associated With

In October 2019, she posted a YouTube video titled "SWITCHING GIRLFRIENDS WITH RUNIK FOR 24 HOURS!! Went Terrible."
