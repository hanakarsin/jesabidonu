Makueni woman in trouble for lying to school principal DP Ruto would attend fundraiser

A Makueni woman claiming to be a close friend of Deputy President William Ruto was on Tuesday, April 16, remanded for giving false information about the DP.

Jane Nduku Makau was accused of lying to Unoa Primary school headteacher that the DP was coming to preside over a function at the school.

A report by Daily Nation showed Makau told the headteacher, Phillip Musyoki Kiema, that Ruto was going to preside over a fundraiser at the school on April 7, knowing too well it was not true.

Musyoki is report to have lined up pupils on April 7, 2019 to entertain the DP only to realise later in the evening there was actually no such function.

Her case will be heard on May 8, 2019.
