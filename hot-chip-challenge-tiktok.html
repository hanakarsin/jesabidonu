Paqui's &quot;One Chip Challenge&quot; Is Heating Things Up on TikTok Here's How to Participate

The "One Chip Challenge" (or "Hot Chip Challenge" on TikTok) was started by tortilla chip brand Paqui after it created the "world's hottest chip."

Another day, another insane TikTok challenge, am I right? The "Hot Chip Challenge," also known as the '"One Chip Challenge," is currently heating things up on the platform. If you're a brave soul who wants to put their spice tolerance to the test, we've got all the challenge deets. Or if you're a weakling like myself (I'm already sweating!) and just want to watch some epic reaction videos, we've got those, too.

What is the "One Chip Challenge" (aka the "Hot Chip Challenge")?

Tortilla chip brand Paqui is the mastermind behind the "One Chip Challenge," which also goes by "Hot Chip Challenge" on TikTok. The snack manufacturer, which uses real peppers in its products, has always been known for its tangy and zesty chips. But in 2016, it decided to take things up a notch and used Carolina Reaper peppers to create the world's hottest chip. According to PepperHead, the Carolina Reaper pepper is the hottest pepper in the world.

Because of the pepper's extreme zest, the brand challenged folks to try the new chip and share their reactions on social media with the hashtag #OneChipChallenge. Fortunately, it was a hit and Paqui decided to make it a yearly thing by releasing an annual zesty chip. How do I participate in the "One Chip Challenge"?

The chip is available for purchase on Amazon. Due to the challenge's popularity, the chip may be out of stock in your area. Be sure to check back frequently, as it is always getting restocked.

Once you have your chip, ask a friend to film your reaction. While there is no prize for successfully consuming the chip, if Paqui gets a laugh from your reaction video, it may add you to its Wall of Infamy. To be considered, tag @PaquiChips in your video of two minutes or less on a social media platform of your choosing, and use the hashtag #OneChipChallenge.
