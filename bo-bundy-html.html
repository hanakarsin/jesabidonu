Bo Bundy - Age, Family, Bio

Hip hop artist and rapper who has released a number of popular singles including "Mi Barrio" and "Colorados." The latter song has an official music video that has been viewed more than 500,000 times on YouTube.

Before Fame

He began his career by releasing music independently on Soundcloud.

Trivia

In 2020, he signed with the record label Rancho Humilde. The label now handles all of his distribution. 

Family Life

He was born in Texas.

Associated With

Both he and Slim Thug found fame in Houston's rap scene.
