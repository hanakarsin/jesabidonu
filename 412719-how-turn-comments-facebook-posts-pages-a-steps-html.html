How to turn off comments on Facebook posts and pages in a few steps

Facebook is one of the leading social media platforms in the world. The platform has helped many people in distant corners of the world to connect. It has also made some individuals miserable from hateful and negative comments. Knowing how to turn off comments on Facebook posts can eliminate such trolls and haters.

To keep your social media account private, change your comment settings. Whenever a person comments on your status or picture, this comment is visible on the Facebook feed to that person's friends. It means that more people can see the content you upload on your profile.

How to turn off comments and likes on Facebook posts in a private profile

Turning off comments and likes from a private profile is not possible. However, you can choose to keep your Facebook profile entirely private by changing comment and like settings. You could change the settings so that only your acquaintances can comment on your posts rather than any random Facebook user.

Restrict private profile comments on a browser

Follow the procedure below to restrict commenting to your acquaintances using a browser:

1. Log into your Facebook account: Connect to the internet, go to the official Facebook login page, Provide your password and phone number or email to log in.

2. Go to settings: On a browser, tap on a drop-down arrow icon located at the top right of your screen.

3. Go to "Settings & Privacy": You will see this option once you have opened your menu.

4. Tap settings: This will lead you to a new window.

5. Choose "Public Posts": The option will be located on the left side of your screen.

6. Change comment settings: Under "who can comment on your public posts?", choose Friends or Friends of Friends.

7. Don't forget to do the same for your profile picture: Below the above options, set "Who can like or comment on your public profile pictures and other profile info" to Friends or Friends of Friends.

How to restrict comments on the phone

If you are using the official Facebook app or Facebook Lite in your Android or iPhone, follow the procedure below:

1. Log into your account: Open the application in question and sign in using your email address, phone number, and password.

2. Go to the Menu: You can do so by tapping on the icon.

3. Choose Settings: Scroll down to see this option.

4. Go to "Public Posts": Scroll to the privacy tab and click on "Public Posts."

5. Change comment settings: Set your Friends or Friends of Friends as the people allowed to comment on your posts. Do the same for your profile picture and other profile information.

Restrict group comments via a browser

Use the steps below:

1. Log into your account: Open your browser, connect to the internet, and head to the official Facebook login page. Provide your login details and tap on the Login button.

2. Tap Groups: Go to your menu, then to Groups.

3. Choose the group in question: Under the "Groups you manage" tab, choose the specific group you want to control comments.

4. Go to Settings: This option can be found on the left side menu on your screen.

5. Restrict commenting: First, hide your group from the general public by making it private and hidden. Second, restrict who can post on the group. Some posts might bring unwanted comments, and you don't want that.

Restrict group comments on the phone

On your mobile phone, follow these steps:

1. Open the Facebook app: Go to your app tray and scroll until you see the application. Click on its icon to open it.

Restrict comments on a Facebook page using a browser

Use the steps below:

1. Log into your account: Go to the official Facebook login page. Provide your correct login credentials.

2. Go to Pages: Tap on the Menu icon and then the Pages option.

3. Tap on the page in question: You will see the pages you manage under the "Your Pages" tab.

4. Go to Settings: On your right-hand side menu, scroll down until you see this option. Tap on it.

5. Moderate commenting: You can edit your page visibility from public to unpublished. You can also restrict visitor posting, restrict people from sending you a direct message, and tagging the page.

Use the steps below to block comments for a single post:

1. Identify the post: The chances are that you are already logged into your account. If not, do so via the Facebook sign-in page. Next, locate the post you want to turn off commenting.
