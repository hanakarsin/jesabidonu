YouTuber Elodie Zone has revealed that she struggles with mental health.

The bubbly media personality shared a video, shedding tears while talking about depression.

Elodie recently broke up with Nviiri the storyteller after dating for almost a year, and it seems things haven't been easy for her.

In a lengthy Instagram post, Elodie said her life is not perfect and has been about battling depression for a while now.

"I recently lost someone close to me and it’s shattered me. I made this when I wasn’t in the right state of mind then went back and forth trying to decide if I should post it or not. I’ve finally decided to because I feel like sometimes I make everything in my life seem perfect online when in reality I’m just as human as you. I get sad, a lot," the YouTuber wrote in part.

She went ahead to narrate how she has been struggling with depression since she was 14, thanking her fans for being supportive.

"I struggle with my mental health, a lot. Truthfully, I have no idea what I’m even doing half the time... but all of you that follow and support me are the reason I keep going. I feel like I’m not fighting for myself, but for this huge family online that may not have anyone else who’s fighting for them. I’ve been extremely open about my struggle with depression since I was 14, so for anyone going through the same- I love you," she said.

Elodie has been using her social accounts to talk about mental health, trying to help many who are not doing well, but she has taken a break from doing that.

"The racing thoughts, the need to blame yourself, the physical chest pains, the numbness. We’re in this together because there’s no other way out than through supporting each other. I took a pause from Mental Mondays cause I felt like I was lying telling you to “be okay” when I was far from it. I’ll be more open about my situation in the next episode," she concluded.

In October 2020, Nviiri and Elodie Zone were spotted together several times getting cosy, and before they spoke out, the cat was already out of the bag.

Speaking in an interview with TUKO.co.ke, Nviiri said he never knew he would date someone in the limelight and wanted his relationship to be private.
