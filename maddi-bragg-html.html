Maddi Bragg - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube phenomenon who runs the beauty and fashion channel madisenrosebeauty1 for more than 1.1 million subscribers. She has a second channel called Maddislifee that has more than 250,000 subscribers. She has also posted lots of content documenting her cooking and what she eats everyday. 

Before Fame

She created her madisenrosebeauty1 beauty channel on April 18, 2011 and her Maddislifee channel on November 30, 2011.

Trivia

She was also popular on Vine, finishing with more than 75,000 followers. She left high school to enter an online school program. She has been associated with the Select management company.

Family Life

She has an older sister and older half-brother. She introduced her mom in a YouTube video in 2014. 

Associated With

She is close friends with fellow YouTube beauty guru Amanda Steele.
