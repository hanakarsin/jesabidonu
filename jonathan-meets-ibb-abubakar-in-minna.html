Jonathan Meets IBB, Abubakar In Minna

Former President of Nigeria, Goodluck Jonathan on Thursday visited the former military head of state, Ibrahim Badamasi Babangida and Abdulsalami Abubakar.

Jonathan meets with the military leaders at their uphill residence in Minna, Niger State.

The former president while addressing newsmen after the meeting described the visit as a regular one to the former Elder Statesmen.

He said, “The visit is just the normal regular one especially being the youngest of all the former leaders.

“It is also an opportunity for me to visit General Abdusalami Abubakar who has just come back from treatment abroad and also took time to visit General Babangida.”

2023 Presidency: Peter Obi Meets Goodluck Jonathan In Abuja

The presidential candidate of the Labour Party, Peter Obi, has met with former Nigerian President, Goodluck Jonathan, in Abuja.

Naija News reports that the former Governor of Anambra State made this known in a tweet alongside photos from the meeting via his verified Twitter handle on Thursday.

The LP presidential flagbearer said he and Jonathan exchanged views on national issues and an array of serious national interest questions during the meeting.

Obi visited the former President at the Goodluck Jonathan Foundation headquarters in the Federal Capital Territory (FCT) on Wednesday.
