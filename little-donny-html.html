Little Donny - Age, Family, Bio

Irish YouTube gamer who focuses on kid-friendly Minecraft content for his more than 1 million subscribers. He is one of many members of The Little Club. 

Before Fame

He launched his Little Donny - The Prince of Minecraft YouTube channel in October of 2015. His first video was "Minecraft - Prince Adventure - LITTLE KELLY MEETS A SECRET PRINCE!" 

Trivia

He did a series on YouTube called Bendy's Life that featured some of his most popular videos, one of which earning over 3 million views. 

Family Life

His real name is Coner Power. His brothers and sisters play Minecraft as well. He married fellow YouTuber Baby Leah in 2018.

Associated With

He is joined in The Little Club along with Little Kelly, Little Carly, and many others.
