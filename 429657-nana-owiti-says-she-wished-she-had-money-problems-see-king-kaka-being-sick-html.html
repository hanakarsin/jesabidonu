Nana Owiti Says She Wished She Had Money Problems than See King Kaka Being Sick

  • Nana disclosed that King Kaka's sickness made her value health more than wealth
  • According to Nana, she is better off dealing with financial challenges than sickness
  • These, Nana said while discussing materialism in marriages and what is best; money or good health

Rapper King Kaka's wife Nana Owiti has disclosed that good health is way better than wealth.

Good health over wealth

Nana, speaking during her show Chatspot on Switch TV, disclosed that she would rather go through challenges financially than being sick or having to deal with a sick member of her family.

These, the mother of two said while talking about materialism in relationships.

According to Nana, King Kaka's illness is one of the lowest moments she has ever dealt with in her entire life.

Nana said she saw life on a different pedestal when her hubby became sick and was undergoing treatment for a very long time.

"As much as we are living in a very material world, let me tell you, when you base your family values on finances, that is like the priority of what your marriage is all about. You may be struggling financially but that should not be your main focus. For me, I just want to live confortably even without money. As long as I have wealth in health," Nana said.

Kaka's illness an eye-opener

The TV host added that it got to a point she wished she was suffering financially than having to see her man suffer in sickness.

"For me, seeing King sick for that long really drained my soul. I was even calling my friend and would tell her that I wish I was struggling with finances but not health. When you are healthy, you are able to solve financial problems, but when you are not, it is totally different," she added.

King Kaka's Wife Nana Discloses Why She Tastes Rapper's Food Before He Eats

Just recently, Nana disclosed she was forced to become King Kaka's connoisseur when the rapper became sick.

Nana noted that a lot of things in their lives changed including the foods the rapper eats and how he eats.

In a social media post seen by TUKO.co.ke on Nana's Instagram page just recently, the mother of two disclosed that they rarely eat in hotels.

According to Nana, they, specifically Kaka can only eat at a hotel where food had been prepared following her prior orders and directions on how it should be cooked.

Nana went on to reveal they most eat at home or at King Kaka's mother's home where they are sure of how the food has been prepared.

The TV host went on to add that she also tastes all the food the rapper eats before he takes them to make sure no spices had been used and to confirm just how it is supposed to be made for Kaka.
