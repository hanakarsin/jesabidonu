Davido early life and musical career

Davido was born on 21st November 1992 in Atlanta, Georgia in the United States. A few years later, his family moved to Nigeria, where he spent most of his childhood life.

His parents are the late Mrs Vero Adeleke, a professor, and Dr Deji Adeleke, a wealthy Nigerian entrepreneur with several business ventures.

Davido attended the British International School, located in Lagos, for his high school studies. After that, he left the country for his university education at Oakwood University in Alabama, in the United States.

The now musical genius pursued a Business Administration course at the institution. However, midway, he dropped out to focus on his passion, music of course.

Later, his father persuaded him to enrol for a music degree at Babcock University, Ilishan- Remo, Ogun State. Davido, without hesitation, honoured his father’s call. The lad, eventually, obtained a Bachelor of Arts Second Class Upper degree qualification in 2015.
