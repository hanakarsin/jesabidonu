Evee Bennett - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube family vlogging star known as one of eight vloggers on the channel The Ohana Adventure. Appearing with her parents and five siblings, the channel has earned more than 3.7 million subscribers. 

Before Fame

She began her YouTube career in December 2015 when her mother launched The Ohana Adventure channel. 

Trivia

One of the most popular The Ohana Adventure videos she appears in is the May 2016 video "KINDERGARTNER CUT HER OWN HAIR." The theohanaadventure instagram account has gained over 400,000 followers. 

Family Life

Her full name is Evelin Bennett. She is one of four daughters and two sons born to her parents, Rachel and Jase. 

Associated With

Her five siblings include sisters Klai, Rykel, and Cora and brothers Shae and Wyatt. They all appear alongside her on The Ohana Adventure.
