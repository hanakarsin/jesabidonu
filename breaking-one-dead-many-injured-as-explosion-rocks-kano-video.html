One Dead, Many Injured As Explosion Rocks Kano [Video]

One person has been confirmed died and many others injured during an explosion on Aba Road, Sabon Gari area of Kano State.

Naija News gathered that the explosion occurred on Tuesday morning in the area.

Footage from the sad incident showed that the blast affected a building with at least one corpse seen on the scene.

The number of casualties is still unknown, but many people were reported dead and several sustained degrees of injuries.

After the blast, some people were seen trying to help the injured and others lamenting the damage.

It is not yet clear if one of the buildings housed a school, but the visuals showed several injured pupils and other schoolchildren being moved away from the scene.

Details of the explosion are still sketchy as of the time of filing this report.
