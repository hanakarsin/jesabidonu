Troy Becker - Age, Family, Bio

Social star and actor who is best known for his troybeckertiktok TikTok account, where he primarily posts short, situational comedy videos. He often makes his sketches with friends or he plays multiple characters. He has amassed over 200,000 fans on the platform.   

Before Fame

He first gained fame on TikTok when the app was known as Musical.ly, earning over 7 million fans on the platform before disabling his account in 2017. He then re-emerged on social media in 2019, with the new name of Troy Becker.

Trivia

He has posted headshots and stylized pictures of himself alongside inspirational captions on his troybeckerinsta Instagram. His page has garnered over 40,000 followers. 

Family Life

He has a sister. 

Associated With

He often features fellow social star Jamie Rose in his TikToks.
