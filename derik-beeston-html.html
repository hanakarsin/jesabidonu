Derik Beeston - Age, Family, Bio

YouTuber known for The Beeston Fam YouTube channel, which features his wife Sarah and their son Jack. The channel has earned more than 1.4 million subscribers. 

Before Fame

He launched the channel in November 2019.

Trivia

On June 14, 2021, he and his wife posted a prank video using a bald filter. They use the channel to post life updates, as well as offer Q&A sessions and detailed accounts of such milestones as how they met. In the spring of 2022, they moved out of Hawaii, and documented the experience through a series of YouTube videos. 

Family Life

In November 2020, he and his wife shared a video about the labor and delivery of their baby.

Associated With

He shares his YouTube channel with his wife Sarah Beeston.
