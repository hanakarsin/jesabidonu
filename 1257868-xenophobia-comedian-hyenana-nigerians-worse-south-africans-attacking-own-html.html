Xenophobia: Comedian Hyenana says Nigerians are worse than South Africans for attacking their own

- Comedian Hyenana has fumed on social media over the reaction of some Nigerians to xenophobia

- Some Nigerians took to looting and destroying of South African owned businesses ever since news of Nigerians being attacked emerged

- It was gathered that other Nigerians used the opportunity to wreak havoc on fellow Nigerians

- Hyenana expressed disappointment at the actions of these Nigerians, saying that Nigerians are worse than South Africans

Ever since the news of Nigerians being attacked in South Africa emerged, some Nigerians took to protest at South African owned businesses to retaliate against xenophobia. However, some went from peaceful protest to destruction and looting.

Nigerian comedian Hyenana has taken to social media o condemn the actions of Nigerians who have been destroying and stealing from South African owned businesses. The comedian noted that Nigerian citizens are the ones working at the places being destroyed.

Hyenana, who condemned Nigerians on posts shared on Instagram, noted that he believes that Nigerians are worse than South Africans because the South Africans were not hurting their own people.
