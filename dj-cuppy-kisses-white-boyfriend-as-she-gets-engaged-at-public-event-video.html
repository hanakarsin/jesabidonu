DJ Cuppy Kisses British Boyfriend As She Gets Engaged At Public Event (Video)

Nigerian female disc jockey and billionaire’s daughter, Florence Otedola, professionally known as DJ Cuppy, is engaged.

Naija News reports that this is coming barely six months after her sister, Temi got engaged to Nigerian singer, Mr Eazi.

Cuppy while congratulating her sister and the singer on social media, had urged the love birds to find her a man because she was also part of their love story.

However, the female disc jockey has finally said yes to her white boyfriend at a public concert, outside Nigeria.

In the video making the rounds online, DJ Cuppy, who seemed surprised when her boyfriend popped the big question, said yes and followed it with a passionate kiss on stage.

Cuppy was also seen hugging the unidentified white man as the audience screamed and clapped in excitement.

Every time I See Tems Picture I Get Confused

Meanwhile, popular Nigerian singer Inetimi Timaya Odon known by his stage name Timaya has praised his younger colleague, Tems for her talent and hard work.

The singer while speaking during an interview on Cool FM tagged “Road show”, shared his thoughts on the new crop of Nigerian singers today.

The ‘Cold outside’ crooner hailed younger artists, Rema, Fireboy, Tems, Ayra Starr, Ruger, and Buju amongst others.

Speaking further he stated that Tems reminds him of a young girl who was a fan and used to come in front of his house just to see him.

He explained that every time he sees her picture he is confused if she is the one or just a lookalike.

He went further to share a short story about Tems, and spoke on how seeing her pictures on social media confuses him.
