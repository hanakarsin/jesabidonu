Kevin Paredes - Age, Family, Bio

Midfielder known for playing with D.C. United in Major League Soccer (MLS). He has been known as a young rising star in the league. In January 2022, he joined VfL Wolfsburg.

Before Fame

He joined the D.C. United's youth league in 2016 before being moved up to play with Loudoun United in the USL Championship league in 2019. He joined the D.C. United in 2020. 

Trivia

He scored his first professional goal in July 2021 against Toronto F.C. in a 7-1 win. 

Family Life

He was born in South Riding, Virginia, United States

Associated With

He is similar to other D.C. United players such as Ben Olsen and Bryan Namoff.
