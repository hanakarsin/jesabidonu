That Mexican OT - Age, Family, Bio

Rapper and hip-hop recording artist who is known for songs such as "Johnny Dang," "Kick Doe Click," and "La Cobra." His musical popularity has led to him amassing more than 1.1 million followers on his verified Instagram account. 

Before Fame

He began to make waves in the music scene with the release of his 2020 LP South Texas Project. 

Trivia

In June of 2023, he boasted more than 2 million monthly listeners on his verified artist page on Spotify. His music videos and live performances have been streamed more than 389 million times on YouTube. 

Family Life

He was born in Texas. He has a dog named Bushwick.

Associated With

He collaborated with Paul Wall and DRODi on the track "Johnny Dang" and he featured Lefty Sm on the song "Barrio."
