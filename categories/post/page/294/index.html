Senate Takes Decision On CBNs Naira Redesign Plan

published on February 17, 2024
The Nigeria Senate on Wednesday resolved to provide legislative support for the policy of the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) to redesign the naira. Naija News reports that CBN Governor, Godwin Emefiele, had on October 26, announced the plan to redesign the Naira to control the money supply. According to him, the redesigned notes include N200, N500, and ₦1,000.
