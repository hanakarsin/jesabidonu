APC Convention - Governors To Meet Today Over New Date

published on June 24, 2024
All is currently not well within the ruling All Progressive Congress (APC) as governors will meet today on the fate of the party’s national convention date, a source revealed. The caretaker committee is said to have proposed a ‘plan’ to present to President Muhammadu Buhari tomorrow Tuesday after reaching a conclusion today with the hope of postponing the convention to March 12, 2022, Naija News reports.
