IGP Egbetokun Visits NSA, Nuhu Ribadu (Photos)

published on March 23, 2024
The acting Inspector-General of Police, Kayode Egbetokun on Tuesday met with the National Security Adviser (NSA), Mallam Nuhu Ribadu. According to the Force Public Relations Officer, CSP Olumuyiwa Adejobi who made this known in a statement, the meeting is part of efforts to strengthen internal security and improve synergy among security institutions in the country. He added that the meeting which took place at the Office of the National Security Adviser, Three Arms Zone, Abuja is to consolidate on the gains recorded so far in the internal security architecture of the nation.
