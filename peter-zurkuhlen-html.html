Peter Zurkuhlen - Age, Family, Bio

Gained fame as the husband of 13 Reasons Why actress Tommy Dorfman. 

Before Fame

He met Tommy Dorfman at a housewarming party held by their mutual friends. 

Trivia

He attended the 2018 Human Rights Campaign Gala Dinner in Los Angeles with Tommy. 

Family Life

He and Tommy became engaged in April 2015 and were married in Portland, Maine, in November 2016. In February 2022, the couple announced that they had filed for divorce.

Associated With

After marrying into the Dorfman family, he became cousin-in-laws with The Bachelor star Andi Dorfman.
