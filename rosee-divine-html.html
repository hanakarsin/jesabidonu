Sophie Brussaux - Age, Family, Bio

Known as both Sophie Brussaux and Rosee Divine, she is a former adult entertainment performer who made waves in the news in 2017 when she was seen dining with Drake in Amsterdam. She has graced the pages of several glamour magazines and would appear in many music videos.

Before Fame

She studied French literature, business and finance at a French institute before moving to America to pursue modeling.

Trivia

She is also an artist. Her painting and print art is influenced by pop art, combining elements of surrealism and symbolism.

Family Life

She was born and raised in France. In 2018, it was revealed she had a son with Drake, whom they named Adonis Graham.

Associated With

Drake went to dinner with her around the time he was thought to have been possibly dating Jennifer Lopez.
