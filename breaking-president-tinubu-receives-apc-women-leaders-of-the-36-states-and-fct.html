President Tinubu Receives APC Women Leaders Of The 36 States And FCT

President Bola Tinubu on Thursday received women leaders of the All Progressives Congress (APC) across the 36 states in Aso Rock.

As of the time of filing this report, the aim of their meeting has not been disclosed.

The meeting could be with respect to the demands of women to be included in the cabinet of President Tinubu.

Shettima Meets UN Deputy Sec Gen. Amina Mohammed, Malala, Others

In another development, Vice President Kashim Shettima, on Wednesday, received a delegation from the United Nations led by the Deputy Secretary General and former Minister of Environment, Amina Mohammed.

Shettima met with the delegation which included Pakistani education activist and Nobel Peace Prize winner, Malala Yousafzai, at the Presidential Villa.

Speaking during the courtesy visit, Shettima said President Bola Tinubu remains fully committed to issues of girl-child education and gender empowerment in the country.

The vice president said the Tinubu government will promote girl-child education and gender empowerment in its policies and programmes under his watch.
