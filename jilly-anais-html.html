Jilly Anais - Age, Family, Bio

Rising pop artist with a debut single called "The Juice," which was a part of her debut EP of the same name. The EP was co-written and produced by the LA Production duo The Invaders. On March 31st, she released her debut EP, COPY & Paste which features production from Lil Rich and Lab Ox.  

Before Fame

She was named Miss Teen Houston in 2011. She was a competitive cheerleader for five years and a high school varsity cheerleader for four years.

Trivia

Her resume includes modeling, acting, commercials, print ads, competitive dance, and competitive cheer.

Family Life

She grew up with a younger brother in Houston, Texas and moved to Los Angeles, CA in 2014. She dated NBA guard Dejounte Murray up until late 2018. She then began dating NFL quarterback Deshaun Watson in 2019. 

Associated With

Jilly released singles "NEW SAFE" and "Different Rules" in 2021 which were produced by Lil Rich. Lil Rich has produced for a number of artists including Bia, Machine Gun Kelly, DaBaby, Jazz Cartier and YG.
