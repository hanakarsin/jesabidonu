Peter Obi Discloses What Nigeria Is Lacking

The Labour Party presidential candidate, Peter Obi has promised to deliver the country back to Nigerians if he gets elected in 2023.

Speaking at the Labour Party and Coalition for Peter Obi Leadership summit in Abuja on Thursday, the LP flag bearer promised Nigerians that his government would grant power back to the civilians.

He pointed out that Nigeria lacks leadership at the moment but his administration, if elected will provide that.

The former Anambra state governor said that the reason he is interested in becoming the next president of the country is to give Nigerians good leadership.

Obi said: “Our job is to take power from those who have it and give it back to you. We have a country called Nigeria but we don’t have Nigerians. What we want to do is to create Nigerians.

“The only way to create Nigerians is to make people have hope in Nigeria and the only way they will have faith in Nigeria is that there is hope in Nigerians.

“The only thing that is lacking in this country is leadership. And that is what we intend to provide. We are not trying to do something else. Leadership is what Datti and I want to provide. We are not looking for anything”.
