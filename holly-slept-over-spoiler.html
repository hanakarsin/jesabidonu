What Happened with Audra and Holly?

'Holly Slept Over' Is a Comedy With a Seriously Sexy Twist

By Brittany Vincent

Updated May 14 2020, 10:43 a.m. ET

Looking for a new romantic comedy flick with some bite? You should consider checking out Holly Slept Over, a 2020 film that follows a married couple looking to spice up their sex life. 

Sure, it's another movie based on the idea that marriage spells a death sentence for a couple's love-making experience, but this one has a fun twist that should delight and surprise anyone looking for something new to enjoy that belongs to the genre. 

If you're uninterested in the exact specifics of Holly Slept Over and want to check out the film for yourself, you might want to stop reading here, as we're going to be going into MAJOR spoilers. 

For anyone else ready to see what type of antics happen, read on. We're going to discuss the film as well as some of the interactivity between characters. Then, you know, if you're digging it, you should totally go check it out. You probably have a little bit more time these days, anyway. 

'Holly Slept Over' spoilers: What's going on with Holly?

Holly Slept Over follows a couple, Audra (Britt Lower) and Noel (Josh Lawson), who are trying to conceive. Their sex life leaves a lot to be desired, and they're looking to spice things up a bit. Things take a turn for the dramatic, especially when Audra admits she once slept with her college roommate, Holly ((Nathalie Emmanuel). This sparks something in Noel, who believes it could be the key to salvaging their intimate relationship. 

Things get really intense, however, when Holly makes a surprise visit to Noel and Audra's home. Holly and Audra immediately hit it off again since they were roommates, but Noel's friend Pete (Ron Livingston) has a suggestion: invite Holly for a threesome with his wife. 

Thus begins the drama, which involves trying to convince Holly to join Audra and Noel for some much-needed spice. 

So, do Audra, Noel, and Holly end up in bed together?

It's intriguing that the movie makes a bold choice to not focus on Holly's thoughts on the matter since she's a third-party to the marriage here, which clearly has several problems as it is. But aside from the sexual tension and the married couples the movie focuses on bickering here and there, Holly Slept Over has a positive message about open and honest communication at its core. It's just couched between a lot of laughs, and if that's what you're looking for, there's plenty to enjoy here. 

In the end, Holly Slept Over is a modest romantic comedy with plenty of twists that should keep most viewers guessing. Will they end up together as Noel desires with a threesome? Or will something wacky and unexpected happen? We won't ruin things for you outright, but keep the premise in mind and see if the movie resonates with you. 

There's a lot going on in the world right now, so we could use a little simplistic narrative and some levity. Even if it comes in the form of a romantic comedy. 

You can watch Holly Slept Over on Amazon Prime Video.
