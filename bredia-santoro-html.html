Bredia Santoro - Age, Family, Bio

Singer, dancer and model who was hired by Kidz Bop to perform across the country. She first appeared on the 25th edition of Kidz Bop along with Grant Knoche, Ashlynn Chong and Matt Martinez.

Before Fame

She was 3 years old when she started singing and dancing. At the age of 6, she started dancing competitively and also began training in classical piano and choral voice.

Trivia

She competed in the Joliet Idol talent competition, a beauty pageant. She was the cover girl for American Girl and also modeled for Kohls.

Family Life

She has a younger brother named Vander and a sister, Trinity.

Associated With

She was joined as a Kidz Bop kid by Jayna Brown on Kidz Bop 26.
