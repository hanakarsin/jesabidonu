Beatrice Chebet wins Kenya first gold medal in women's junior World Cross Championship in 6 years

Youngster Beatrice Chebet once again made history on Saturday, March 30, after clinching Kenya's first gold medal in six years at the World Under-20 Championships in Aarhus, Denmark.

Last year, Chebet inked her name in the books of history after becoming the first Kenyan woman to win the 5,000m title during the World Under-20 Championships.

However, unlike her past races, Chebet's victory on Saturday had to be decided after a review following protestations from Kenyans.

Ethiopia's Alemitu Tariku had initially been declared winner, with Tisigie Gebreselama settling for silver in a photo-finish.

But it later emerged Chebet had breast-chested the tape first to be handed victory in a photo-finish.
