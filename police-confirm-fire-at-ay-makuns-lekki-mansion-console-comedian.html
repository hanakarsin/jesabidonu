Police Confirm Fire At AY Makuns Lekki Mansion, Console Comedian

The Nigeria Police spokesperson, Olumuyiwa Adejobi, has consoled with comedian AY Makun after raging fire gutted his Lekki mansion on Sunday.

Although AY is yet to speak on the incident, the comic actor and producer took to his Instagram page on Monday to indicate he and his family are safe.

The movie star also implied that his wife and children are currently in the United States.

AY, who appeared to be currently in Alberta, Canada, said he could not wait to join them.

“Family and God. That is what’s important. Everything else comes and goes. Can’t wait to join you guys in the US in a few days,” he wrote.

Adejobi via his social media on Monday wished AY well in a post on Instagram.

“I sympathise with my brother, AY Comedian, on the fire incident that gutted his house. It’s quite unfortunate. It’s well with you, bro, ile oba tojo, ewa lo maa busi. May the Lord be with you,” he wrote.
