Blaster Silonga - Age, Family, Bio

Lead guitarist of the Filipino indie band IV of Spades known for singles such as "Mundo" "Hey, Barbara," and "Ilaw sa Daan." He has over 30,000 followers on his blastersilonga Instagram account. 

Before Fame

His father, Allan "Daddy A" Silonga, actually founded the band and also serves as their manager.

Trivia

His parents decided to homeschool him to keep up with his rigorous schedule as a musician. In late 2021, he went solo, and announced he signed with the label Island Records Philippines. Shortly after, he dropped his first solo song, titled "Disko Forever."

Family Life

He has three brothers named Dave, Ashanti, and Alleinah. 

Associated With

He's joined in IV of Spades with Badjao de Castro.
