Yammy - Age, Family, Bio

British gamer, vlogger, and blogger formerly known as Yammy xox who primarily plays the game Minecraft on her Yammy YouTube channel. She has amassed over 4.1 million subscribers there for her Let's Plays and gaming commentaries. 

Before Fame

She launched her channel as ChicksCanGame on April 29, 2011. 

Trivia

She has a second YouTube channel called missyammyx where she posts Haul videos as well as fashion related content. 

Family Life

She has two brothers as well as a half-brother. She has a son named Dante whom she had when she was in high school. She married fellow gamer Koil1990 in 2017. 

Associated With

She has made videos with fellow British Minecraft gamer LDShadowLady.
