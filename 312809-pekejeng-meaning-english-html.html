As slang continues to take root in most urban societies in Kenya, finding Pekejeng meaning is just one of the ways one can keep themselves updated. It is especially very important for people living in or near ghettos to understand the basic forms of the communication. You do not want to be left out in what would be a meaningful conversation just because you cannot respond or understand the slang.

Kenyans are getting accustomed to the 'Sheng' language. While the meanings or usage of certain words may vary from one locality to another, there are certain names whose meanings are similar and cut across the board. The popularity of this language has become so significant that it has necessitated the creation of a sheng dictionary and other forms of translations. So, what is Pekejeng meaning in Sheng?

The meaning of Pekejeng

Pekejeng song by Sailors has recently been hitting the radio waves hard with different stations playing the tune and many fans requesting for it. But just what does Pekejeng mean? According to the Sheng Kamusi or dictionary, the word means dance style. However, there are so many other meanings of the name according to different quarters.
