Legendary actor Bill Cobbs, who featured in more than 200 films and TV shows according to his IMDb profile, has passed away at the age of 90, as confirmed to Distractify by his rep, Carmela Evangelista of Evangelista Talent Management. Bill, who was known for roles such as Reginald from Night at the Museum and Master Tinker from Oz the Great and Powerful, initially had his death announced on June 26, 2024, by a family member on Facebook.

With such an abundance of iconic roles, fans are devastated to hear the news of his sad passing, and now many want to know exactly what happened.

Bill Cobbs died at home with his family on June 25, 2024.
