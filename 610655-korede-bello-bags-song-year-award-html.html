Korede Bello Bags Song Of The Year Award

Korede Bello is one of the rave of the moment in the music industry as he has bagged several awards from his hit single 'Godwin'. 

Korede won ‘The Song of the Year’ award at the 4th Annual Golden Icons Academy Movie Awards (GIAMA) that held last night in Houston, Texas.

The event was hosted by Nigerian comedian and actor AY Makun.

Korede Bello released his follow up single, ‘Somebody Great’ about a week ago and the song has since gathered massive air play and acceptance for the messages inherent in it.

On 28 February 2014, Korede got signed to Mavin Records under which he has recorded popular songs like "African Princess" and "Godwin". He is currently working on his first studio album and cites Asa and 2face Idibia as people he draws inspiration from.
