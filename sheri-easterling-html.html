Sheri Nicole - Age, Family, Bio

TikTok star who is best recognized for being the mom of fellow social star Addison Rae. She is known for sharing her own TikTok content featuring dancing and lip-syncing videos for her own 13 million followers on the app. Her TikTok username is sherinicolee. In January 2020, she signed with the talent agency WME. 

Before Fame

She first started appearing on TikTok as a guest on Addison's account. She then created her own in August of 2019 and shared a viral video with her daughter earning over 800,000 likes. 

Trivia

She shares travel photography through her Instagram and has traveled to destinations such as Panama City, Concan Texas, and Aruba. In 2020, she and Addison began co-hosting a podcast called Mama Knows Best. She is the matriarch of The Rae Family on TikTok.

Family Life

Her last name is Easterling. Nicole is her middle name. In addition to Addison, she has two sons named Lucas and Enzo. She often shares photos alongside her husband Monty Lopez on Instagram. 

Associated With

She shared a photo with actor Kirk Cameron at the First Baptist Church in Haughton on her Instagram account in May of 2017.
